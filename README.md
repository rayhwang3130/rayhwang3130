### Profile
I specialize in shaping and delivering AI-enabled solutions that support better business decision making. My work typically spans the full lifecycle of an initiative: identifying high-value use cases, structuring the analytical problem, designing the technical architecture, building deployable proofs of concept, and translating outputs into tools that stakeholders can actually use.

My recent work has focused on conversational analytics, VoC intelligence, automated labeling pipelines, and enterprise AI workflows built on cloud-native data infrastructure.

### Areas of Focus
- AI strategy and use-case development
- End-to-end solution architecture for applied AI
- Conversational analytics and agentic systems
- VoC and qualitative/quantitative insight workflows
- Cloud-based data and AI implementation on GCP
- Decision-support tools for business stakeholders
